EAT HEALTHY

Foods to Avoid in Your Gym Diet Plan

 Artificial trans fats in baked goods and fast food raise cardiovascular risks. Simple carbohydrates in sugary snacks cause rapid blood sugar spikes, leading to energy crashes and hindering exercise performance. Moderating alcohol intake is crucial as it hampers recovery and muscle-building processes. Opting for healthier fats and complex carbohydrates supports fitness goals and overall well-being, reducing the risk of chronic diseases.
